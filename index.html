A Letter to Maan

This is our very first Eid together, Maan, and somehow the moon looks different tonight. It feels warmer, softer, as if even the sky knows that something beautiful has found its way into my life. That something is you.

Before you, I did not know that a single person could make ordinary days feel like celebrations. But here we are, on our first Eid, and I am grateful in ways I do not have enough words for.

I want every Eid of yours from this moment to be full of the same warmth you give to everyone around you, especially the warmth you give to me without even trying.
